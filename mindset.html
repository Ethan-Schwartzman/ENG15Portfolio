Growing Intelligence

Dear Future Student,

The change from life as a high school to college student is a new and exciting experience. Yet despite the joy of this transition, there are a new set of challenges that come with college. It is no question that academics are rigorous and demanding. There may come a time where you feel that you are falling behind and unable to meet the fast pace of classes. It is important to understand that struggling does not make you incapable. In fact, it is a part of the learning process. The brain is like a muscle, it gets stronger with regular use. It may not be easy at first, but continuing to practice, persevere, and overcome these challenges will allow you to develop as a student.

Best of luck,

Ethan Schwartzman
